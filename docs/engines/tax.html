Partnership Tax · Form 1065

Returns and K-1s, prepared for your CPA's review

Partnership tax is allocation arithmetic at scale: every partner, every layer, every capital account rolled forward correctly. This engine normalizes your books, computes allocations, and drafts the Form 1065 and a K-1 for every partner — tied to the ledger, with an evidence binder behind every figure. Your CPA reviews and files; nothing files itself.

What customers ask

Does it replace my CPA?

No — it does the assembly a CPA shouldn't spend nights on. The draft arrives review-ready with every number traceable, and your CPA keeps filing authority.

How are allocations verified?

Every allocation is recomputed by independent logic and capital accounts must roll forward — if they don't tie, the draft doesn't advance.

What about many partners or special allocations?

Per-partner K-1 drafting is the core of the engine — each K-1 ties back to the ledger and the allocation schedule that produced it.
