Cash & Debt Reconciliation

The full population of accounts — not a sample — matched and explained

Sampling is a compromise. This engine reconciles the full population — every cash and debt account, GL against bank and lender records, line by line — and explains every exception it finds, classified by materiality. It even traces cash out of closed and migrated accounts, the places where balances usually go to disappear.

What customers ask

Why full population instead of sampling?

Because the account you didn't sample is where the surprise lives. Full-population matching costs an engine nothing — and gives your auditors an evidence log that covers everything.

What counts as an exception?

Anything that doesn't tie between GL and the bank or lender record — classified by materiality, explained in plain language, and held for your sign-off.

Can it handle closed or migrated accounts?

Yes — tracing cash across account closures and system migrations is a first-class feature, not an edge case.
