Validation Engine

Read-only workbook review with PASS / REVIEW / FAIL verdicts

A second pair of eyes for finished work: the Validation Engine reviews workbooks read-only — formula integrity, lineage, cross-sheet consistency — and returns PASS / REVIEW / FAIL verdicts with a byte-identical guarantee that it never modifies what it audits.

What customers ask

Will it change my file?

Never — the engine is read-only by construction and proves it: the reviewed workbook is byte-identical before and after.

What triggers a REVIEW verdict?

Findings that are real but judgment-dependent — the engine flags and explains them rather than guessing.
