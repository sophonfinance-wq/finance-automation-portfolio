Self-healing operations

The engines run the control loop themselves.

Each engine watches its own output, corrects routine drift from source data, re-verifies every correction against an independent recomputation, and escalates the judgment calls to a person. Nothing self-certifies.

SOURCEGL · bank · schedules
COMPUTEFinance engine
SEPARATE LOGICIndependent recompute
DECISIONTie-out
Ties → posts with evidenceWhen the independent check confirms the work, it posts with a written audit trail.
Routine break → fixed from sourceA mis-keyed cell or dropped intercompany leg is corrected, then re-verified before it counts.
Judgment call → a personWhat the loop cannot prove is quarantined and flagged — never rubber-stamped.

You set the policy: full autonomy in approved environments, or a human sign-off on every post — the controls and audit trail are identical either way. Live today on five engines; every loop writes its own run report, with sample reports committed on GitHub.

Controls & safety

Built to make your auditor comfortable.

Wherever code can enforce a control, it does — inspectable in the public repository. Where it can't, the commitment goes into the engagement terms, in writing.

C-01

Evidence first

Every computed figure carries a citation to its source — file, sheet, row. No orphan numbers.

C-02

Independent verification

A separate pass with separate logic recomputes the work. The preparer never grades itself.

C-03

Completeness & separation of duties

Populations reconciled in full; preparer, reviewer, auditor, and human gate are distinct roles in the software.

C-04

Human approval

Nothing posts, sends, or files itself. Your people hold final sign-off on every output.

C-05

Your environment, your data

Engines run inside your environment or one you approve. Outputs, workpapers, and documentation are yours.

C-06

Refusal & audit trail

Out-of-tie entries cannot post and unverified reports do not ship — and every run writes a log of what it read, computed, and corrected.

Client engagements are confidential. All public demonstrations run exclusively on fictional, seeded data.
