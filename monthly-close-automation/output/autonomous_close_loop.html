Why it's defensible

Autonomous does not mean ungated — the gate is a deterministic, logged policy instead of a person. The loop never invents a number: every correction is the engine's own re-derivation from the seeded sub-ledger of record, booked as an adjustment. The ten controls remain the acceptance test; the loop only posts once they fall silent over the non-quarantined scope. A broken opening carryforward it will not fabricate; a signed-off locked period it will not overwrite — both are held and logged. The verdict doubles as a CI exit code.
